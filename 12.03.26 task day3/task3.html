CSK Won The Match

The Chennai Super Kings won the 2023 Indian Premier League title after defeating Gujarat Titans in a thrilling final. Led by captain MS Dhoni, CSK chased a revised target of 171 in 15 overs. In the final two balls, Ravindra Jadeja hit a six and a four to seal the victory. This dramatic win earned CSK their fifth IPL trophy, matching the record for the most titles in IPL history.
